B.A. Maddux is an author and web developer currently residing in Omaha, Nebraska.

What if you weren’t born? What if you were made?
Made for a purpose.
Infiltration, espionage, seduction.
Thievery.
In the streets of Aeonston, there are two types of people. Those you can’t trust and those who are only out for themselves.
And when you’re a vat-born genetic chimera – a true lab rat – on a mission to capture the breath of a dragon, knowing who to trust is everything. But when everything about you is artificial, can you even trust yourself?
